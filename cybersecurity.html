Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from cyber threats. In today's interconnected world, it has become a vital pillar for both individuals and organizations.

Key Areas of Cybersecurity

Network Security

Protecting networks from intrusions, malware, and unauthorized access through firewalls, VPNs, and monitoring systems.

Data Privacy

Ensuring sensitive information is encrypted, anonymized, and stored securely to comply with regulations like GDPR.

Incident Response

Detecting, mitigating, and recovering from security breaches with well-documented response plans.
